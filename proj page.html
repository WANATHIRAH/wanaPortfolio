UITM Jasin Non-Resident House Rental System is the official platform for non-residents students at UiTM Jasin to find their rental houses and help any landlord who wants to rent out their houses. This system also will be monitored by the admin which is Jawatankuasa Perwakilan Non-Resident (JPNR).
